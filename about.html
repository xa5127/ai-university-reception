About the AI Reception

The AI University Reception is a smart digital assistant designed to help students, staff, and visitors access important academic and campus information instantly. It works as a 24/7 virtual front desk, free from human wait times or office hours.

Purpose of the AI Reception

Our goal is to centralize university information and make it easier for students to find answers without searching multiple sources. The AI Reception reduces confusion, improves communication, and offers quick, accurate guidance on academic processes and services.

  • Answer common student questions instantly
  • Reduce waiting time at physical reception desks
  • Guide students to the correct departments
  • Provide COOP, advising, and campus support information

What Students Can Ask

  • Tuition, fees, and finance questions
  • Course information and prerequisites
  • COOP requirements and steps
  • Campus navigation
  • Faculty contact details
  • IT support and troubleshooting
